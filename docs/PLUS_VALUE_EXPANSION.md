# BA-65–69 Breakout Plus Value Expansion

Breakout Plus is the complete privacy-first recovery system. Breakout Plus AI
inherits all Plus value and adds optional secure AI personalization.

## BA-65 — Daily Recovery Dashboard

- Private daily risk snapshot
- First safe action
- Current or next risk window
- Recommended routine
- Seven-day activity snapshot
- Direct access to Rescue, logs, routines, and deeper tools

## BA-66 — Private Pattern Intelligence

- Peak risk day and four-hour time range
- Repeated triggers and trigger-plus-reason combinations
- Cycle-stage or mood signals recorded before slips
- Victory evidence showing what has helped
- Current-week versus previous-week direction
- Deterministic weekly summary calculated on-device

## BA-67 — Advanced Recovery Plan Builder

The free core first-action plan remains available. Plus adds:

- Early warning signs
- Primary triggers
- High-risk times
- Morning and evening commitments
- Post-slip rebuild plan
- Review date
- Plan readiness
- Richer recovery reports and accountability summaries

## BA-68 — Programs and Education

- Seven structured programs from 7 to 30 days
- Nighttime, stress, loneliness, trigger-awareness, rebuilding, foundation,
  and optional Christian programs
- Nine-plus premium education tracks
- 25-plus premium lessons
- Private on-device progress

## BA-69 — Accountability Center and Scorecard

- Check-in preparation
- Trusted-person or group name
- Check-in date
- Win, risk, support request, and next commitment
- Reviewable copyable summary
- Consent-based links to sharing controls
- Recovery engagement score based on preparation and participation
- No punishment, worth judgment, purity score, or slip-reset streak
