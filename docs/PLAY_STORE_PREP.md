# Breakout Addiction Play Store Prep

## Current app posture

Breakout Addiction is Android-first and CI-built. The app should be treated as demo-ready, not production-submission-final, until the store listing, privacy policy, data safety answers, screenshots, and final subscription decisions are reviewed.

## Demo-ready areas

- Home recovery dashboard
- Rescue flow
- Cycle awareness
- Mood and recovery logging
- Insights
- Education
- Support planning
- Privacy settings
- Feature controls
- Optional AI shell and guardrails
- Widget preview and app-entry simulation
- Demo readiness and About Breakout screens
- Privacy & Safety Center
- Release Readiness screen

## Store review checklist

Before Play Store submission, confirm:

1. App name, short description, and full description.
2. Screenshots for phone layouts.
3. App icon and feature graphic.
4. Privacy policy URL.
5. Data safety answers.
6. Content rating questionnaire.
7. Target audience settings.
8. Health/support disclaimers.
9. Subscription/billing decision.
10. AI disclosure language if any remote AI path is enabled.
11. Final Android release APK/AAB artifact from GitHub Actions.
12. Founder/owner approval of all sensitive wording.

## Safety language

Breakout can encourage recovery, guide reflection, and support habit interruption. It should not be described as emergency care, medical treatment, therapy, or a guaranteed outcome.

## AI language

AI features are optional. The non-AI recovery path must remain valuable. Any live remote AI path should be reviewed for privacy, consent, disclosure, safety guardrails, and cost controls before public release.
